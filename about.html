â€‹I am an independent videographer and photographer based out of Columbus, OH with a desire to curate content for clients from all backgrounds. I provide on-site work that allows me to create a client-specific experience that is tailored to you.

In 2017, the sports arena initially drew me to this line of work. I deeply admire the passion and grit that each athlete possesses as they continue to strive for excellence. The dedication they have to perfect their craft inspires me to further perfect mine and adequately capture their hard work and grind, both on the field and behind the scenes. I have extensive experience in videography, photography, and editing for events, teams, and individuals in the sports realm, including professional athletes, various local sports organizations and leagues, trainers around the Columbus area, the 2019 Columbus Destroyers of the Arena Football League, and the Scholastic Play-by-Play Classic held in Columbus at Nationwide Arena.

By 2019, my interests broadened as I became motivated to curate content for individuals, events, corporations, and businesses of all backgrounds. Expanding my horizons has allowed me to work with clients outside of Ohio, in places such as Hawaii, Arizona, The Bahamas, Florida, Chicago, and NYC. I take great pride in the opportunity to generate and brand an image for my clients. My goal is to provide each client with high-quality video and photo content that allows them to market themselves at a higher level than their competitors. I value service and communication, and I work with each client individually to ensure that the delivered content exceeds expectations.
