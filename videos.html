Oasis Dental Care

Oasis Dental Care is a general dentistry office that is owned and operated by Dr. Dan Rodda in Flagstaff, Arizona. I spent two full days in the office filming, interviewing, and exploring Flagstaff and everything that Northern Arizona has to offer.

Malik Harrison

Malik Harrison is a former Ohio State football player and current member of the Baltimore Ravens. Malik was drafted in the 3rd round of the 2020 draft, where he now looks to succeed in his role in the Ravens defense.

Avon Oaks Country Club

Avon Oaks Country Club is a golf course located in Avon, Ohio, located just 25 minutes outside of Cleveland, Ohio. I spent two full days capturing film and taking photos of members of the country club.

Play-by-Play Classic

The Play-by-Play Classic is an event that was held at Nationwide Arena in the winter of 2019 that showcased some of the best talent in the state of Ohio. Sierra Canyon High School based out of California also made the trip, which rosters Bronny James; Lebron James' son.

Brian Asamoah Workout

Brian Asamoah is a sophomore linebacker for the Oklahoma Sooners and is an alumni of St. Francis De Sales High School located in Columbus, Ohio. Brian is looking to make his mark this season as he pursues excellence in becoming one of the best defensive players on Oklahoma. I was able to capture one of his off-season training sessions before he left for the season.

Skills Development University

Skills Development University is an off-season, winter workout organization that trains high school football players in Columbus, and surrounding areas. SDU is a 10-week program that offers a wide variety of training techniques, drills, and one-on-ones for anyone that wants to participate. Skills Development University was created by Derick Alexander and David Mason as an opportunity for kids to keep busy during the football off-season.

Frisco Fighters Team Practice

The Frisco Fighters are a professional football team based out of Dallas, Texas. The Frisco Fighters compete in the Indoor Football League and 2020 was their inaugural season. I was able to take a trip to Texas alongside Bottomline Content to film, and help them prep for their season.

Wesson Brothers Workout

Andre and Kaleb Wesson are two brothers that played basketball for Ohio State from 2016-2019. Both of them were tremendous players for the Buckeyes during their time with the program.

Smiles at Sea Vendors

Smiles at Sea is an annual cruise cruise put on by Elijah Desmond and his team. Smiles at Sea is packed with over 600 dental professionals from all over the United States, who earn CE credit towards dentistry, as they attend different classes and events throughout the week. In 2019 I was able to join the Smiles at Sea team and document their events, as we sailed the Atlantic Ocean and visited Key West and The Bahamas.
